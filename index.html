Technopark: A Gateway to Innovation

Welcome to Technopark Trivandrum- where ambitions take flight amidst a perfect blend of work & life. Nestled within a serene 768- acre oasis in Thiruvananthapuram aka Trivandrum - Kerala’s dynamic capital city; this premier high-tech hub not only boasts state-of-the-art  IT infrastructure alongside collaborative co-working spaces, but has also set the bar high for sustainable development, IT breakthroughs and employee well-being. To top it, this IT Park in Trivandrum is not only the first IT Park in India but is also India’s largest green technology park. Technopark had ignited Kerala’s IT revolution and also set the tone for India’s broader technological transformation and advancement.

A Hub of Excellence

The State of Kerala, acclaimed globally as “God’s Own Country,” is well known for its educational excellence, outstanding healthcare standards, rich tourist destinations, and model social cohesion. Thiruvananthapuram, home to Technopark, enjoys the reputation of being the alluring doorway to “God’s Own Country,” blending a rich cultural heritage with the forward-thinking ethos of innovation that Technopark represents.

Technopark Trivandrum is designed as a business park to spark growth, fuel aspirations and foster innovation that sync up with nurturing the welfare and progress of businesses and their workforce.

More than A launch pad to Global Business Expansion 

 

Being the bellwether IT Park in the country, Technopark Trivandrum has been a trendsetter in overhauling Kerala’s IT business environment since the 1990s. While progress is central to this Innovation Park in Kerala, Technopark’s journey soars beyond offering cutting-edge facilities to industry leaders and future game-changers. Therefore Technopark will be more than a launchpad for your businesses because this greenest IT Park in India is designed to unfold as the central hub from where all your groundbreaking decisions and business needs will flourish. 

 

 

 

Thiruvananthapuram: A Preferred Destination for Start-ups and Top Businesses

 

Renowned for its exceptional quality of life, Thiruvananthapuram city is a confluence where natural beauty blends with modern convenience. Graced with scenic surroundings, the city rightfully boasts world-class healthcare, prestigious educational facilities and invigorating fresh air. Technopark set in Thiruvananthapuram is a flourishing entrepreneurial environment complemented with all the vital ingredients of a business technology park required to boost innovation, collaboration, growth and success. Sheltering and nurturing an extensive range of companies, Technopark thrives in positive surroundings matched by exceptional talent, minimum turnover rates, robust infrastructure solutions, and cost-effectiveness offering the best business environment and IT office space in Kerala. These features make Technopark the preferred IT infrastructure solution not only for established businesses but also for start-ups. 

 

 

Our Advantage: Versatility and Flexibility

 

Technopark Trivandrum is a treasure trove of options and opportunities. Irrespective of whether you are a start-up or a top organisation; simplicity and clarity are the hallmarks of all procedures adopted at this IT Park in Trivandrum. Our institution has fostered an industrial configuration through which micro, small and medium-sized businesses are enabled to grow alongside leading businesses. We offer flexible and fully equipped shared-office space in Kerala with all modern facilities designed to support the growth and expansion of small businesses. For larger organisations looking to expand, Technopark provides a range of options including customisable leases that cater to various needs and stages of business development.

 

Not just an IT Park in Trivandrum, but a Visionary Tech Location.

 

When business strategy and innovation merge to become dynamic partners, doors to new avenues get opened for businesses to stay ahead of the competition. Such dynamic partnerships provide challenging markets for skilled talent because skilled talent is fundamental for businesses to grow and maintain a competitive edge.  On this score, Trivandrum has a readily available skilled talent pool especially for IT sector businesses seeking top-notch professionals. That’s because, as remote and hybrid work models become the norm notably in the IT sector, and professionals increasingly choose to return to their hometowns, Thiruvananthapuram is experiencing a surge in skilled talent looking forward to rewarding IT careers. The distinct advantage in this context is for businesses operating from locations that do not fall within congested Indian cities. Technopark Thiruvananthapuram enjoys this advantage.  Apart from offering exceptional strategic value, our IT Park in India has additional advantages like low operational costs with a lively business landscape- and this unique combination remains unmatched.
